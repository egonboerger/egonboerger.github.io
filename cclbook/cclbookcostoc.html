Egon Börger: Introduction into the Theory of Computation.

A Register Machine Approach.

HyperCOSTOC-Course (COmputer Supported Teaching Of Computer science).
10 Lectures (2100 KBytes) developed with AUTOOL + handbook (735K, pp.334).
Edited with assistance of R.Bellucci, G.Pulice, E.Vannini, R.Gherardi.
Hofbauer, München 1989.

Electronic introductory course (with accompanying textbook in printed form) containing a streamlined version (and adaptation to the electronic medium) of the following selected themes from the Computability, Complexity, Logic book
  • While programs and recursive functions
  • An interpreter for While programs
  • Church's thesis and algorithmically undecidable problems
  • Enumeration and recursion theorem
  • Recursion theorem: applications
  • Finite automata 1
  • Finite automata 2
  • Modular decomposition of automata
  • A small universal Turing machine program
  • NP-completeness
